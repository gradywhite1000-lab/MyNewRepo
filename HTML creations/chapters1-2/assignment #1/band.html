Favorite CD Releases & Reviews

The New Abnormal
Personally my favorite album by the alternative masterminds. Produced by legendary sound engineer Rick Rubin, it features a blend of their classic sound with a new twist, highlighting their creativity
First Impressions of Earth
For me this album is their most dark sounding studio creation. Not a bad thing the guitars really standout and take the lead for the sound as a whole.
Angles
A more intresting spin in sound they've done. More bright and catchy tunes. Some of my favorites are Under Cover of Darkness and Taken for a Fool.
